The pipeline

Six steps. One click.

Everything between the inbound email and the outbound quote happens automatically — the agent just reviews and sends.

01
Paste email

Agent drops the raw customer email into the form.

02
Extract

Routes, dates, pax, cabin and preferences are parsed out.

03
Detect gaps

Missing info is flagged — or sensible defaults are assumed.

04
Fetch pricing

Live fares via pricing adapters (Amadeus, Hotelbeds…).

05
Apply policy

Markup, fare rules and hold windows applied automatically.

06
Draft reply

A polished, ready-to-send quotation — agent approves.

Capabilities

Built for the way
agents actually work.

No new inbox, no workflow to relearn. QuoteAssist sits beside the tools your team already uses and does the tedious part.

Requirement extraction

Origins, destinations, dates, passenger mix, cabin class and special requests parsed straight from free-text email — no forms for the customer to fill.

Missing-info detection

Spots what the customer forgot — return date, room count, budget tier — and either flags it for the agent or applies a safe default, transparently.

Live pricing adapters

A mock adapter returns realistic fixtures in development; real providers — Amadeus, Hotelbeds and more — plug in for production with no UI change.

Policy engine

Markup rules, supplier preferences, fare hold windows and compliance text applied to every quote — so juniors and seniors price the same way.

Professional drafts

On-brand, itemised quotations in your house tone and currency — copy to clipboard, or send straight from the integration. Agent always approves first.

History & audit

Every quote is stored with its source email, extracted fields, pricing snapshot and the agent who approved it — searchable and re-openable.

Email input · roadmap

From paste, to add-in,
to fully hands-off.

The quotation engine stays the same — only how the email reaches it changes as you roll out.
